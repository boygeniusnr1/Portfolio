# Clothes Swap Society

Clothes Swap Society is a digital hub for a circular fashion economy. The project focused on translating a physical, token-based exchange system into a streamlined digital discovery and coordination platform.

### Core Objectives
*   **Decentralize Growth:** Empower local leads to host their own neighborhood swaps using an integrated toolkit.
*   **Accessibility:** Design a mobile-first discovery engine for users to find and register for "Closwaps" on the go.
*   **Sustainability Branding:** Create a clean, minimalist aesthetic that reinforces the mission of environmental and financial sustainability.

### Technical Contributions
*   **Event Workflow Logic:** Digitized the rules for item intake, quality control standards, and token distribution to ensure consistent event quality.
*   **Engagement Modules:** Built volunteer recruitment and lead capture systems to fuel community expansion.
*   **Performance Optimization:** Delivered a lightweight architecture optimized for fast load times and high accessibility, ensuring zero friction for users at physical event locations.

### Impact
The platform serves as the primary operational engine for the Society, successfully scaling circular fashion events and reducing textile waste through a highly organized, community-centric model.
